• Free-Weather.com

    With just a few lines of code you can offer your visitors visual climate graphs for over 2000 cities around the world. Very handy for travel, sports and other general portals.

    • Frequency: Occasional
    • Cost: Free
    • Revenue Bearing: No
    • Country Bias: International
    • Links: Website

  • Intellicast

    A nice selection of graphic live weather forecasts, radar images and road conditions for your site.

    • Cost: Free
    • Revenue Bearing: No
    • Country Bias: International
    • Links: Website

  • Real Conditions

    If you have a sport or snow activity related site, then this content is for you. You can add the current ski conditions for virtually any ski resort in the world. If they don't have it then you can add it. WAP content is also available.

    • Cost: Free
    • Revenue Bearing: No
    • Country Bias: International
    • Links: Website

  • The Weather Network

    Specialised Canadian weather content for your website. Very nice looking graphical weather buttons showing the current weather in any Canadian city you specify or a map of canada showing current conditions.

    • Cost: Free
    • Revenue Bearing: No
    • Country Bias: Canada
    • Links: Website

  • OnlineWeather.com

    Finally, some detailed weather that is not US based. This excellent site has a number of very stylish looking free Java weather applets that scroll the UK and Irelands weather forecasts in a very nice looking interface. There are 3 free options available and a number of low cost ones for a more customised requirement.

    • Cost: Free and some low cost
    • Revenue Bearing: No
    • Country Bias: UK
    • Links: Website
